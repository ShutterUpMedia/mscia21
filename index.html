Rules for Paper Presentation

1).Each team has minimum of 1 and maximum of 3 members .
2).Each team will be given 5 minuets for presenting their ideas.
3).No participants can be a part of more than one team.
4).The decision of judges will be final and no arguments or appeal will be entertained.

Rules for Debbuging

1).The event is only for solo participants.
2).Totally two rounds will be conducted.
3).Top five participants will be selected for final round.
4).For First round all should share their entire screen.
5).For second round the selected participants should share their entire screen and also enable their video mode.
6).The challenge link will be posted using the chat box of the meet.

Rules for Programming Wizard

1).Only one participant.
2).Totally 5 questions will be asked.
3).2 questions are easy and 3 questions are difficult.
4).The first 2 questions 15 minutes is given. If the person not able to solve those 2 questions are unable to attend the next 3 questions. The link will be sent in chat box.
5).The last 3 questions will be given 45 minutes.
6).Late submission is not considered.
7).All the participants must share your screen.
8).Laptop is mandatory.
9).Any kind of malpractices the participant will be disqualified.
10).Any language can be used for programming.
11).HackerRank account is mandatory.
12).HackerRank account should be registered with kongu.edu mail id.

Rules for Quiz

1).Only one participant per team is allowed to attend prelims.
2).6 teams will be shortlisted from prelims.
3).3 rounds will be conducted.

Rules for Multimedia

1).Trailer Creation:
* Video clippings of 20 minutes will be given.
* From the given clippings ,create a trailer of 2 minutes.
* You can also use video clippings of the same movie another clips what we have given.
* Plagiarism is strictly prohibited.
2).Image Manipulation:
* Series of random five images will be given.
* Using those images, blend them all into a single picture .
* Plagiarism is strictly prohibited.
3).Poster Making:
* Create a poster on a given theme.
* The Poster canvas can be in any size.
* Plagiarism is strictly prohibited.
